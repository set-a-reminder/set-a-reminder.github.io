Privacy Policy for "Set a Reminder" Extension

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Contact Email: vladrix.cws.1@gmail.com

1. What Data Does the Extension Collect?

The "Set a Reminder" extension collects and processes only the following data:

  • Reminder data: Text, date, time, and completion status of reminders that you consciously create.
  • Google authorization data: Standard OAuth 2.0 tokens to connect with your Google account.

The extension DOES NOT collect names, addresses, phone numbers, payment information, geolocation, or browser/device data.

2. How Is Your Data Used?

  • For synchronization: Your reminders are synced with your personal Google Calendar so you can see them on all your devices.
  • For local storage: Reminders are stored locally in your browser for offline functionality.

Data is used exclusively to provide reminder functionality and for no other purposes (marketing, analytics, selling, etc.).

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

We DO NOT share your data with anyone. The only exception is data transmission to Google (via the official Google Calendar API), which is necessary for the extension to function and occurs only at your request.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on your device (browser local storage) and in your personal Google Calendar. We have no servers that store user data.

5. Your Rights

You can delete all extension data at any time by disabling synchronization in settings or by removing the extension from your browser. To delete data from Google Calendar, follow standard Google instructions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any changes to this policy through an extension update in the Chrome Web Store.
